## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Is my data safe?**
A: Yes! We use industry-standard security. However, use test data during beta as we may reset the database.

**Q: Can I make real purchases?**
A: Not yet. Payments are in test mode. Real transactions will be enabled at launch.

**Q: When will [feature X] be available?**
A: Check the **Announcements** in the app for our roadmap and release dates.

**Q: Can I invite friends?**
A: Yes! You'll receive invite codes to share with up to 5 friends.

**Q: What happens to my account after beta?**
A: Your account will carry over to the public launch. You'll keep your beta tester badge!

**Q: I found a security issue, what should I do?**
A: Email `security@yourdomain.com` immediately. Do not post publicly.

**Q: Can vendors sell real products during beta?**
A: No, all transactions are test-only during beta. Real selling starts at launch.
